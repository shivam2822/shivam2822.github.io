The Trkr App (Tile Management App)

Key Features:
  • Inventory Management: - The app offers a centralized database to track tile inventory, including details like type, color, size, quantity, and location. - Barcode scanning functionality enables easy addition and tracking of tiles as they arrive in the warehouse. - Real-time updates help manage stock levels and prevent overstocking or shortages.

  • Customer Support Integration: - Integrated customer support ticketing system allows customers to raise concerns, inquiries, or issues related to their tile orders. - AI-powered chatbot offers instant responses to common queries, providing 24/7 assistance. - Customer support agents can access relevant order and inventory information to provide accurate assistance.

  • Live Chat for Updates: - Customers can engage in real-time chats with support agents to receive updates on their orders, delivery status, or product availability. - Push notifications and alerts keep customers informed about order progress.

  • Order Tracking and Notifications: - Customers receive notifications at each stage of their order, from processing to shipping and delivery. - GPS tracking enables customers to monitor the delivery of their tile orders in real time.

  • Analytics and Reporting: - Generate reports on inventory levels, popular tile types, customer inquiries, and order fulfillment efficiency. - Data-driven insights aid in making informed decisions about stock replenishment and customer service improvements.
